Contestants need to reach 400 votes to qualify as finalists. Keep encouraging your supporters to vote!

Can I vote multiple times?

Yes! You can purchase as many voting packages as you want to support your favorite contestant.

When will votes be added?

Votes are typically added within 24 hours of payment verification. Please be patient during busy periods.

What if I made a mistake?
